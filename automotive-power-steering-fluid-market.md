<p><p>Automotive power steering fluid can be categorized into two main types: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oil is a man-made fluid that offers better stability at different temperatures and provides improved lubrication for the power steering system. On the other hand, mineral oil is derived from natural sources and is typically less expensive than synthetic oil. Both types of power steering fluid serve the purpose of reducing friction and wear within the steering system, ultimately improving the overall performance and longevity of the vehicle.</p></p>
