The market for medical adsorbents can be classified into three main types: synthetic resin, activated carbon, and polysaccharide. Synthetic resin adsorbents are widely used due to their high efficiency in removing toxins and impurities from the body. Activated carbon adsorbents are known for their large surface area and high adsorption capacity. Polysaccharide adsorbents are gaining popularity for their biocompatibility and low toxicity, making them suitable for use in medical devices and drug delivery systems.</p></p>
