<p><p>Automotive rear-view camera systems are categorized into two main types: wired and wireless. Wired rear-view camera systems are connected directly to the vehicle's electrical system and require more complex installation. On the other hand, wireless rear-view camera systems use a wireless connection to transmit video feed to a screen in the vehicle, making installation easier. Both types offer the benefit of enhancing driver visibility and safety while reversing or parking. The choice between wired and wireless systems depends on factors such as installation ease and the specific needs of the vehicle owner.</p></p>
