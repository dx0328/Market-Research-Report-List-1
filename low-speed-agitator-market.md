<p><strong>The Low Speed Agitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anchor Impeller</li><li>Helical Impeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anchor impeller and helical impeller are two types of low speed agitators used in various industries. Anchor impeller is designed with blades that resemble an anchor, providing high shear and mixing at the bottom of the tank. Helical impeller, on the other hand, consists of twisted blades that promote gentle mixing and are suitable for applications requiring low shear. Both types are commonly used in pharmaceutical, chemical, and food processing industries for efficient mixing and agitation processes.</p></p>
