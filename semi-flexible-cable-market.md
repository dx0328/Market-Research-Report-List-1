<p><p>Semi Flexible Cable market can be divided into two types - Type I Semi Flexible Cable and Type II Semi Flexible Cable. Type I cables are more rigid and less flexible compared to Type II cables. They are commonly used in applications where precise positioning and minimal movement is required. Type II cables, on the other hand, offer higher flexibility and are often used in applications where frequent bending and movement is expected. Both types cater to different market needs based on their specific characteristics.</p></p>
