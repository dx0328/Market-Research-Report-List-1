<p><strong>The Automotive Hydraulic Buffer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Tube</li><li>Double Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Hydraulic Buffer Market Types include Single-Tube and Double Tube buffers. Single-Tube buffers have a simpler design with one tube containing the hydraulic fluid and a piston inside, providing damping during compression and rebound. Double-Tube buffers have two tubes - an inner tube for the piston and hydraulic fluid, and an outer tube that acts as a reservoir. This design allows for better heat dissipation and improved performance in various driving conditions. Both types play a crucial role in providing smooth and controlled movement in automotive suspension systems.</p></p>
