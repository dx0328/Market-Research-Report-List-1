<p><strong>The Wheel Detection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wheel Sensor</li><li>Double Wheel Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>A Wheel Detection System is a device used to detect the presence and movement of trains by sensing the wheels of the train. There are two main types of Wheel Detection Systems: Single Wheel Sensor and Double Wheel Sensor. </p><p>Single Wheel Sensor systems use one sensor to detect each passing wheel of the train, while Double Wheel Sensor systems use two sensors to detect both wheels of each bogie. Both systems are commonly used in railway applications to ensure the safe and efficient operation of trains.</p></p>
