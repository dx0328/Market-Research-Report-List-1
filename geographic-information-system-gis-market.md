<p><p>The Geographic Information System (GIS) hardware market includes devices such as GIS Collectors, Total Stations, and LIDAR systems. GIS Collectors are portable devices used to collect and update geospatial data in the field. Total Stations are surveying instruments used for measuring angles and distances. LIDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) systems use laser technology to create precise 3D maps. On the other hand, the software market involves applications and programs that help process, analyze, and visualize geospatial data. These software solutions enable users to manage and manipulate geographic information effectively.</p></p>
