<p><ul><li>Single Vision Prescription Glasses</li><li>Progressive Prescription Glasses</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The prescription glasses market can be divided into two main types: single vision and progressive glasses. Single vision glasses have a consistent prescription across the entire lens, suitable for correcting either nearsightedness or farsightedness. On the other hand, progressive glasses have multiple focal points within the same lens, allowing for seamless transition between near and far vision. Both types cater to individuals with vision impairments, with single vision glasses being more common for straightforward prescriptions and progressive glasses offering a more sophisticated solution for those needing multifocal correction.</p></p>
