<p><p>Terahertz imaging inspection market typically consists of passive and active Terahertz imaging technologies. Passive Terahertz imaging involves detecting natural radiation emitted by objects, while active Terahertz imaging requires an external source of radiation to illuminate the objects. Passive imaging is often used for security screening, while active imaging is more commonly utilized for material analysis and quality control in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, automotive, and electronics. Both technologies play a crucial role in non-destructive testing and imaging applications.</p></p>
