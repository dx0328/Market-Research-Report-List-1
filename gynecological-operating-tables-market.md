<p><p>Gynecological operating tables come in two main types: height-adjustable and fixed-height. Height-adjustable tables can be raised or lowered to accommodate the preferences of the surgeon and provide optimal positioning for the patient during procedures. On the other hand, fixed-height tables have a set height that cannot be adjusted. While height-adjustable tables offer more flexibility, fixed-height tables are generally more cost-effective and easier to maintain. Both types play a crucial role in providing a stable and comfortable platform for gynecological surgeries.</p></p>
