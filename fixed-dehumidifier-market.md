<p><p>Fixed dehumidifiers are classified into different types based on their functioning. Refrigerant dehumidifiers use refrigeration technology to cool the air and extract moisture. Desiccant dehumidifiers use a desiccant material to absorb moisture from the air. Ventilated dehumidifiers work by constantly circulating air to remove excess moisture. Other types of fixed dehumidifiers include hybrid dehumidifiers, which combine features of both refrigerant and desiccant models. Each type caters to different needs and environments, providing effective moisture control solutions.</p></p>
