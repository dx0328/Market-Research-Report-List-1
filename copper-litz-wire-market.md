<p><p>Copper Litz Wire market is segmented into two types: Directly Bunched Type and Multi-step Bunched Type. Directly Bunched Type involves twisting the individual copper strands together in one step, resulting in a compact and dense structure. On the other hand, Multi-step Bunched Type involves twisting the individual strands in multiple steps to create a larger surface area for improved conductivity. Both types offer different advantages and cater to diverse requirements in various industries such as telecommunications, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
