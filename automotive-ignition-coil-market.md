<p><strong>The Automotive Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ignition coil market consists of two main types: single-spark and multi-spark. Single-spark ignition coils produce one spark per ignition cycle, providing reliable and consistent ignition. On the other hand, multi-spark ignition coils produce multiple sparks per ignition cycle, leading to improved fuel efficiency and performance. Single-spark ignition coils are typically used in traditional combustion engines, while multi-spark ignition coils are becoming increasingly popular in modern vehicles for enhanced performance and efficiency.</p></p>
