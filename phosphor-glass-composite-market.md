<p><p>The Phosphor-Glass Composite Market can be categorized into three main types: YAG Phosphor, PIG Phosphor, and Others. YAG Phosphor refers to the use of yttrium aluminum garnet as the phosphor material in the composite. PIG Phosphor, on the other hand, stands for phosphor-in-glass, where the phosphor material is embedded in a glass matrix. The category of Others encompasses all other types of phosphor materials used in the composite. Each type offers unique properties and applications within the market.</p></p>
