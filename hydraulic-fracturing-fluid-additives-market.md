<p><p>Hydraulic fracturing fluid additives market includes various types such as corrosion inhibitors, biocides, crosslinkers, surfactants, and others. Corrosion inhibitors protect equipment and pipelines from rust and deterioration. Biocides prevent the growth of bacteria and other microorganisms in the fracking fluid. Crosslinkers help maintain the viscosity of the fluid, while surfactants reduce surface tension and improve fluid flow. Other additives may include friction reducers, scale inhibitors, and pH adjusters to optimize the performance of the fracking process.</p></p>
