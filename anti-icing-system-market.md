<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Anti-icing System market is divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I systems are designed to prevent the formation of ice on the surface by continuously releasing de-icing fluid. On the other hand, Type II systems focus on removing ice that has already formed by applying de-icing fluid similar to Type I systems, but also utilizing pneumatic systems to break and remove the ice. Both types are crucial for maintaining safe conditions in various industries such as aviation and transportation.</p></p>
