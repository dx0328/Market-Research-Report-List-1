<p><strong>The Rotary Airlock Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drop-Through Rotary Valves</li><li>Blow-Through Rotary Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drop-Through Rotary Valves are commonly used in applications where materials are gravity-fed into the valve and discharged into a conveying system. They are suitable for handling a wide range of materials including powders, pellets, and granules. </p><p>Blow-Through Rotary Valves are designed for pneumatic conveying systems where materials are blown into the valve and then discharged into the conveying line. They are ideal for applications requiring high-pressure differentials and are often used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
