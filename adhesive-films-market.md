<p><strong>The Adhesive Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Hot-melt</li><li>Light-cured</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive films market types include Pressure Sensitive, Hot-melt, Light-cured, and Others. Pressure sensitive adhesive films are easy to apply and require only light pressure for bonding. Hot-melt adhesive films are melted to achieve bonding. Light-cured adhesive films use ultraviolet light for curing. Other types of adhesive films include solvent-based, water-based, and reactive films. Each type offers distinct advantages and is used in various applications based on their specific properties and bonding mechanisms.</p></p>
