<p><p>Ionic Type Electroactive Polymers refer to a group of materials that exhibit electroactive behavior in the presence of ions. The market includes various types such as Ionic Polymer Gel (IPG), Ionomeric Polymer-Metal Composites (IPMC), Conductive Polymers (CP), Carbon Nanotubes (CNT), and others. IPG offers high flexibility and biocompatibility, IPMC combines metal particles with ionomers for actuation, CPs conduct electricity and can change shape, CNTs provide high conductivity and mechanical strength. Other types in the market offer unique properties and applications.</p></p>
