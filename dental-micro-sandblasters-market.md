<p><p>Dental micro-sandblasters are specialized tools used in dental procedures to remove stains, plaque, and debris from teeth. These micro-sandblasters are available in various types, including 2-tank, 1-tank, and other market variations. The 2-tank micro-sandblasters have two separate tanks, one containing the abrasive material (typically aluminum oxide) and the other filled with a cleaning solution. This type allows for efficient cleaning and simultaneous disinfection. Meanwhile, 1-tank micro-sandblasters combine the abrasive material and cleaning solution in a single tank. Other market variations refer to micro-sandblasters with unique features or functionalities that differ from the standard 2-tank or 1-tank designs.</p></p>
