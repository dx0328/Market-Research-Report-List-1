<p><p>Fiberglass pipe market consists of different types like GRE pipes, GRP pipes, and others. GRE pipes, which stands for Glass Reinforced Epoxy pipes, are made with a combination of epoxy resin and continuous glass fiber filament wound in a rotating process. GRP pipes, or Glass Reinforced Plastic pipes, are made with a polyester resin and glass fiber combination. Other types in the market include RTR pipes, FRP pipes, and HDPE pipes. Each type offers specific advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
