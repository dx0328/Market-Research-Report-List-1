<p><ul><li>Solvent-Borne</li><li>Water-Borne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior architectural coatings can be categorized into two main types: solvent-borne and water-borne. Solvent-borne coatings contain volatile organic compounds (VOCs) that evaporate into the atmosphere during application, while water-borne coatings use water as a carrier for the paint. Solvent-borne coatings are known for their durability and ability to provide a high gloss finish, while water-borne coatings are becoming increasingly popular due to their eco-friendly nature and low VOC emissions. Both types of coatings offer different benefits and are used for various applications in the construction industry.</p></p>
