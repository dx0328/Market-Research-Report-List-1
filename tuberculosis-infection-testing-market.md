<p><p>The Tuberculosis Infection Testing Market comprises several types of tests used to diagnose tuberculosis (TB) infection. The Tuberculin Skin Test (TST) involves injecting a small amount of TB protein into the skin and checking for a local reaction. Interferon Gamma Released Assay (IGRA) measures the release of interferon gamma by immune cells in response to TB proteins. Other methods include molecular diagnostics, nucleic acid amplification tests, and serological tests. These tests help identify TB infection, aiding in timely detection and treatment.</p></p>
