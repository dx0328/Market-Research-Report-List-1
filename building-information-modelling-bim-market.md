<p><p>Building Information Modelling (BIM) is a process involving the generation and management of digital representations of physical and functional characteristics of buildings and infrastructure. The integration of BIM with advanced technologies like artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and internet of things is also fueling market growth. Additionally, the shift towards cloud-based BIM solutions and the emergence of BIM software for mobile devices are trends shaping the future of the BIM market. As construction projects become more complex and demanding, the demand for BIM solutions is expected to continue rising in the coming years.</p></p>
