<p><p>PU Films market can be broadly categorized into Polyester PU Films and Polyether PU Films. Polyester PU Films are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for applications that require high abrasion resistance and dimension stability. On the other hand, Polyether PU Films are preferred for their flexibility and elasticity, making them suitable for applications that require stretchability and comfort. Both types of PU Films are widely used in industries such as automotive, textiles, medical, and packaging.</p></p>
