<p><p>The Aircraft Airspeed Indicators market includes two main types: Analog Type and Digital Type. They offer more precise and accurate measurements, along with additional features like trend monitoring and data recording. Digital Type indicators are commonly found in modern and advanced aircraft models.</p></p>
