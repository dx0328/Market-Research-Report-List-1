<p><p>Silicone adhesives come in various types to cater to different needs. High temperature silicone glue is designed to withstand extreme heat and is ideal for applications in high-temperature environments. Room Temperature Vulcanizing (RTV) adhesive can be used at room temperature and cures quickly to form strong bonds. Silicone rubber sealant is commonly used for sealing gaps and joints to prevent leaks. Other types of silicone adhesives include specialty formulations for specific applications such as electronics or medical devices.</p></p>
