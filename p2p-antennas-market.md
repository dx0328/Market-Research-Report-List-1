<p><ul><li>Parabolic Antennas</li><li>Flat Panel Antennas</li><li>Yagi Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>P2P antennas market consists of three main types - Parabolic antennas, flat panel antennas, and Yagi antennas. Parabolic antennas are dish-shaped and are highly directional, making them suitable for long-distance communication. Flat panel antennas are compact and offer a wider coverage area, making them ideal for urban environments. Yagi antennas have multiple elements arranged in a line, providing a good balance of directionality and coverage distance. Each type of antenna has its own strengths and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the communication network.</p></p>
