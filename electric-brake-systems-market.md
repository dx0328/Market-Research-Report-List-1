<p><strong>The Electric Brake Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disc EBS</li><li>Drum EBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Brake Systems (EBS) market consists of two main types: Disc EBS and Drum EBS. Disc EBS utilize pads that clamp onto a rotor to slow down a vehicle, providing better performance and durability compared to Drum EBS. Drum EBS, on the other hand, use shoes that expand inside a rotating drum to stop the vehicle, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types of EBS play a crucial role in enhancing the safety and efficiency of braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
