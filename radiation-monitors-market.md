Portable radiation monitors are handheld devices that can be easily carried around and used to monitor radiation levels in different areas. Transportable dosimetric monitors, on the other hand, are larger and require transportation between locations. These monitors are used to measure radiation doses and help ensure the safety of workers in industries such as nuclear power plants and medical facilities. Both types play a crucial role in monitoring radiation levels and protecting human health.</p></p>
