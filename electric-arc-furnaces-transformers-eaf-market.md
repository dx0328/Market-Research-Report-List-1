<p><p>Electric Arc Furnaces Transformers (EAF) market involves two main types - AC Furnace Transformers and DC Furnace Transformers. AC Furnace Transformers are used in alternating current electric arc furnaces, which are more commonly found in steel production. DC Furnace Transformers, on the other hand, are used in direct current electric arc furnaces, which are typically utilized in non-ferrous metals production. Both types of transformers play a critical role in converting electricity to the appropriate voltage needed for the operation of electric arc furnaces.</p></p>
