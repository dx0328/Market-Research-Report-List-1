<p><p>The Mainstream Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) market consists of several types, including Collaborative Product Definition Management (CPDM), Mechanical Computer-Aided Design (MCAD), Simulation & Analysis (S&A), and Digital Manufacturing (DM). CPDM focuses on collaborative product design and development, enabling teams to work together efficiently. MCAD involves using computer-aided design tools for mechanical engineering tasks. S&A focuses on simulation and analysis of product performance. DM is the application of digital tools and technologies for optimizing manufacturing processes. These types of PLM help organizations streamline product development, improve efficiency, and reduce costs.</p></p>
