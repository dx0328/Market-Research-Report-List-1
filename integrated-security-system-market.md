Cloud-based ISS allows for remote access, real-time monitoring, and scalability, making it a preferred choice for many organizations. The integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms into ISS is another emerging trend. These technologies enhance the accuracy and efficiency of security systems by enabling intelligent video analytics and automated threat detection.</p><p>Another trend impacting the market is the integration of physical security systems with IT networks. This convergence enables better communication and collaboration between different security systems, facilitating a more comprehensive and efficient security approach.</p><p>The growing demand for smart city infrastructure and the increasing adoption of Internet of Things (IoT) devices in various sectors are also driving the growth of the ISS market.
