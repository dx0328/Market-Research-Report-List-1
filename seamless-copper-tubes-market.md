<p><p>Seamless copper tubes are available in various types, including Type K, L, M, DWV, ACR, and others. Type K tubes are the thickest and are typically used for underground applications. Type L tubes are commonly used for residential water supply lines. Type M tubes are thinner and suitable for residential and commercial heating and cooling systems. DWV (Drainage, Waste, and Vent) tubes are designed for drainage systems. ACR (Air Conditioning and Refrigeration) tubes are used for HVAC systems. Other types cater to specific applications in the construction and industrial sectors.</p></p>
