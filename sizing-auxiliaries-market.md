<p><p>Sizing auxiliaries market includes various types such as humectants and co-solvents, surfactants, defoamers, rheology modifiers, and others. Humectants and co-solvents are used to maintain moisture and solubility in sizing formulations. Surfactants help in reducing surface tension and improving wetting properties. Defoamers are added to prevent foam formation during sizing process. Rheology modifiers control the viscosity and flow properties of sizing solutions. Other types include additives like biocides, antioxidants, and chelating agents, which enhance the performance and stability of sizing formulations.</p></p>
