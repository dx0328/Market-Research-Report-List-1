<p><p>PTFE films are available in different types based on their surface properties. Hydrophobic PTFE films repel water and are commonly used in applications where water resistance is important. On the other hand, hydrophilic PTFE films are designed to attract water, making them suitable for applications where moisture absorption is required. Other types of PTFE films may have specific properties or additives tailored for unique applications, such as increased flexibility or enhanced adhesion. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
