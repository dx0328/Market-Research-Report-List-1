<p><p>Sales analytics software can be classified into two main market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises sales analytics software is installed and operated locally on a company's own servers and infrastructure. This type offers the advantage of having complete control and customization over the software but requires high maintenance and upfront costs. On the other hand, cloud-based sales analytics software is hosted and accessed over the internet. It provides scalability, flexibility, and lower initial costs, as it is managed and maintained by the software provider.</p></p>
