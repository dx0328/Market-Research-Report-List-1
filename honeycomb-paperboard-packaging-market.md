<p><p>Honeycomb paperboard packaging is widely used in various applications including shock absorber, bracing, cradling, void fillers, and cushioning. Bracing involves using honeycomb paperboard to reinforce and stabilize products or packaging, ensuring their safety. Cradling refers to the use of honeycomb paperboard forms to securely hold and protect delicate or fragile items. Void fillers are used to fill empty spaces and prevent movement within a package. Lastly, honeycomb paperboard cushions are used to provide additional protection and shock absorption for products during shipping or handling.</p></p>
