<p><strong>The Commercial Air Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vapor Type Air Humidifier</li><li>Water Spray Air Humidifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vapor Type Air Humidifiers use heat to create steam, which is then released into the air to increase humidity levels. These types are generally more effective in larger areas. Water Spray Air Humidifiers work by spraying water droplets into the air, which evaporate and increase moisture levels. They are typically more affordable and suitable for smaller spaces. Both types provide benefits in terms of improving air quality, reducing static electricity, and preventing dry skin and respiratory issues.</p></p>
