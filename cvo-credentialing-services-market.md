<p><p>CVO Credentialing Services market includes various types such as Initial and Re-credentialing, Expirables Management, and Sanctions Monitoring. Initial and Re-credentialing involves verifying and validating the credentials of healthcare providers during the hiring process and periodic renewal. Expirables Management focuses on monitoring and maintaining the expiration dates of licenses, certifications, and other necessary credentials of healthcare professionals. Sanctions Monitoring ensures continuous monitoring of healthcare providers for any disciplinary actions or sanctions imposed by regulatory bodies, ensuring compliance with industry regulations and patient safety.</p></p>
