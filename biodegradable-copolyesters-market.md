<p><p>Biodegradable copolyesters such as PBS (polybutylene succinate) and PHA (polyhydroxyalkanoate) are gaining popularity in the market due to their eco-friendly properties. On the other hand, PHA copolyesters are biodegradable polymers produced by bacteria and are being used in a wide range of applications, including medical devices and food packaging. Both types of copolyesters offer sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics, driving growth in their respective markets.</p></p>
