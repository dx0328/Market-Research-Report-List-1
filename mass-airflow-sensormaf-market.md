<p><p>Mass Airflow Sensor (MAF) Market consists of various types such as Vane Air Flow Sensor, Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor, Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor, and Hot Film Air Flow Sensor. Vane Air Flow Sensor operates based on the movement of a vane, Karman Vortex Air Flow Sensor uses the principle of Karman vortex street, Hot Wire Air Flow Sensor detects airflow using a heated wire, and Hot Film Air Flow Sensor works by measuring the cooling effect of the airflow on a heated film. These sensors play a crucial role in determining the air intake of engines in automotive applications.</p></p>
