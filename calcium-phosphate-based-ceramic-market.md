<p><p>Calcium phosphate based ceramic materials can be classified into two main types in the market: Biological inert ceramic and bioactive ceramics. Biological inert ceramics are materials that do not interact with the surrounding biological environment and remain stable within the body. On the other hand, bioactive ceramics have the ability to bond with living tissue and promote bone growth and regeneration. Both types of ceramics play a crucial role in various biomedical applications such as bone grafts, dental implants, and tissue engineering.</p></p>
