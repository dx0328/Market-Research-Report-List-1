<p><p>UV-LED Light Source market includes three main types: Linear Irradiation, Spot Irradiation, and Surface Irradiation. Linear Irradiation involves uniform exposure along a straight line. Each type has its own applications and benefits, ranging from precision curing in manufacturing processes to sterilization in healthcare settings.
