<p><strong>The Enterprise Payroll Outsourcing Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Managed Outsourcing</li><li>Co-Managed Outsourcing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enterprise Payroll Outsourcing Solutions Market Types can be categorized into two main categories: Full-Managed Outsourcing and Co-Managed Outsourcing. </p><p>Full-Managed Outsourcing refers to a comprehensive approach where the entire payroll process is outsourced to a third-party service provider. This includes all aspects of payroll management, from data entry to tax compliance and payroll processing. The organization hands over complete control and responsibility for the payroll function to the outsourcing partner.</p><p>On the other hand, Co-Managed Outsourcing is a collaborative approach where the organization retains some control over certain payroll processes while outsourcing others. In this model, the organization and the outsourcing provider work together to manage specific tasks or functions, such as data input or tax filing, while sharing responsibilities.</p><p>Both types of outsourcing solutions offer benefits such as cost savings, expertise, and streamlined processes. The choice between full-managed and co-managed outsourcing depends on the organization's specific needs, preferences, and desired level of involvement in payroll management.</p></p>
