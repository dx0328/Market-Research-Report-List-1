<p><p>The aerospace industry pressure sensors market is segmented into different types based on their functionality. Absolute pressure type sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, while differential pressure type sensors measure the difference between two pressures. Relative pressure type sensors measure pressure relative to the atmospheric pressure. Other types of sensors include vacuum pressure sensors and sealed pressure sensors. Each type plays a unique role in monitoring and controlling pressure levels within aerospace systems to ensure safe and efficient operation.</p></p>
