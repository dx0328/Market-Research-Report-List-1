<p><p>Industrial Non-mobile Equipment Hydraulic Cylinders can be categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. Rod cylinders are the most common type and consist of a piston rod that extends and retracts within a cylindrical barrel. Telescopic cylinders, on the other hand, contain multiple nested stages that allow for a longer stroke length compared to rod cylinders. Both types are widely used in industrial applications such as manufacturing, construction, and material handling for providing powerful and efficient hydraulic motion control.</p></p>
