<p><p>The 3-Phase Meter market can be categorized into Half Hourly or Non-Half Hourly Meters based on how frequently the data is collected and reported. Half Hourly Meters collect data every half hour, providing a more detailed consumption profile, while Non-Half Hourly Meters collect data at longer intervals. The Automated Meter Reading (AMR) market involves meters that automatically collect and transmit data to a central system, improving efficiency and accuracy in meter reading. These technologies play a crucial role in modern energy management systems.</p></p>
