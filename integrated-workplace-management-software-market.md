<p><p>Integrated Workplace Management Software (IWMS) is available in two market types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises IWMS is a software solution installed on the company's own servers, allowing for complete control and customization, but requiring significant IT resources and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based IWMS operates on the vendor's servers, accessible through the internet. This market type offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT burden, making it a preferred choice for many organizations. Both types provide comprehensive workplace management solutions, but differ in terms of infrastructure and deployment method.</p></p>
