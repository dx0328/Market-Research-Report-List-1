<p><p>The sterile lap sponges market consists of two types: with X-ray detectable thread/clip and without X-ray detectable thread/clip. The sponges with X-ray detectable thread/clip are designed to be easily visible on X-ray images, allowing healthcare professionals to locate and retrieve them if accidentally left inside a patient after surgery. On the other hand, sponges without X-ray detectable thread/clip do not have this feature, making them more affordable but potentially increasing the risk of retained surgical items. These two variants cater to different needs and preferences within the medical industry.</p></p>
