<p><p>Agricultural colorants are used in the farming industry to enhance the appearance and properties of various agricultural products. These colorants are categorized into two main types: dyes and pigments. Dyes are water-soluble colorants that penetrate and bond with the material, while pigments are insoluble particles that adhere to the surface of the material. Both types of colorants are used in the agricultural industry to color seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and other products for improved aesthetics and visibility.</p></p>
