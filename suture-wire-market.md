<p><ul><li>Absorbable Suture Wire</li><li>Non-Absorbable Suture Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture wire market is divided into two types: absorbable and non-absorbable suture wire. Absorbable suture wire is made from materials that are naturally broken down and absorbed by the body over time. It is commonly used for internal wound closure and reduces the need for additional removal procedures. Non-absorbable suture wire, on the other hand, is made from synthetic materials that are not absorbed by the body. It is used for external wound closure and requires removal after the wound has healed.</p></p>
