<p><p>Medical laminated tube packaging is commonly available in two types: Alumunium Barrier Laminated (ABL) tubes and Plastic Barrier Laminated (PBL) tubes. ABL tubes consist of multiple layers including aluminum which provides excellent barrier properties against moisture, oxygen, and light. PBL tubes are made of different layers of plastic materials and offer good barrier protection as well. Both types of tubes are widely used in pharmaceutical and healthcare industries for packaging creams, ointments, gels, and other medical products.</p></p>
