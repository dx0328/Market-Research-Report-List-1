<p><p>Creosote oil is mainly categorized into two types: wood-tar creosote oil and coal-tar creosote oil. Wood-tar creosote oil is produced by the dry distillation of wood, while coal-tar creosote oil is obtained from the distillation of coal tar. Both types of creosote oil are widely used in various industries such as agriculture, construction, and pharmaceuticals. Wood-tar creosote oil is popular for its antiseptic and insecticidal properties, while coal-tar creosote oil is commonly used for preserving wood and in the production of carbon black.</p></p>
