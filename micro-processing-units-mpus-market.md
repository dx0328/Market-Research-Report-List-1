<p><p>Micro Processing Units (MPUs) are classified into two main market types based on their memory capabilities: Read-Only MPUs and Read/Write MPUs. Read-Only MPUs are designed to only read information from memory, making them suitable for tasks such as data retrieval or display systems. On the other hand, Read/Write MPUs have the ability to both read from and write to memory, allowing for data manipulation and storage functions. These market types cater to different applications depending on the required memory access capabilities.</p></p>
