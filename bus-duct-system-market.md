<p><p>A bus duct system is a type of power distribution system that consists of a set of copper or aluminum bus bars enclosed in a protective housing. It is used to efficiently transmit power from a power source to various electrical loads in industrial, commercial, and residential settings. Bus duct systems are preferred over traditional cable systems due to their high capacity, compactness, and flexibility.</p><p>The bus duct system market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for reliable and efficient power distribution systems. This growth can be attributed to several factors such as the rising construction activities, rapid industrialization, and the increasing need for uninterrupted power supply.
