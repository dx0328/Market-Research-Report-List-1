<p><p>Steel Core Aluminum Stranded Wire (SCASW) comes in various types including All Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced (ACSR), Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced/Aluminum Clad Steel Reinforced (ACSR/AW), and Aluminum Conductor Steel Reinforced/Trapezoidal Wire (ACSR/TW). ACSR is designed for high tensile strength, ACSR/AW combines the conductivity of aluminum with the strength of steel, while ACSR/TW offers increased ampacity. These types serve different purposes based on their specific construction, making them suitable for various applications in power transmission and distribution systems.</p></p>
