<p><p>Avalanche backpacks come in two main types: Mono Avalanche Airbags and Dual Avalanche Airbags. Mono airbags have one airbag that inflates in the event of an avalanche, providing flotation to keep the wearer on top of the snow. Dual airbags have two separate airbags that deploy on either side of the wearer, providing even more stability and protection. Both types of backpacks are designed to help prevent burial in snow slides and increase the chances of survival in avalanche situations.</p></p>
