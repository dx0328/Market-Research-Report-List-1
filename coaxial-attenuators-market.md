<p><p>Coaxial attenuators are devices used to reduce the power of a signal without distorting it. There are two main types of coaxial attenuators: fixed coaxial attenuators and step coaxial attenuators. </p><p>Fixed coaxial attenuators have a set attenuation level that cannot be adjusted, while step coaxial attenuators allow for precise adjustments of attenuation levels in discrete steps. Both types of attenuators are commonly used in various industries such as telecommunications, aerospace, and defense for signal processing and testing purposes.</p></p>
