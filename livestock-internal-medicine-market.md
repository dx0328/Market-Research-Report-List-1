<p><p>Livestock internal medicine market types can be categorized into external use and internal use products. External use products are administered topically or through injections on the outside of the animal's body, while internal use products are ingested or injected into the animal. External use products are often used for skin conditions or external parasites, while internal use products are used to treat internal diseases or infections. Both types of products play a crucial role in ensuring the health and wellbeing of livestock animals.</p></p>
