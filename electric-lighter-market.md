<p><p>The electric lighter market consists of two main types: plasma lighters and electric arc lighters. Plasma lighters use electricity to ignite a small, high-temperature plasma arc, making them windproof and eco-friendly. Electric arc lighters create a small electric arc between two electrodes to generate a flameless heat source. Both types offer convenience, reliability, and reusability compared to traditional lighters. These innovative lighters are gaining popularity due to their effectiveness, durability, and environmentally friendly features.</p></p>
