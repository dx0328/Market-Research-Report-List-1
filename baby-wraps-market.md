<p><p>Baby wraps market types include front wraps and hip wraps. Front wraps are designed to carry the baby in a front-facing position, allowing the caregiver to maintain eye contact and provide optimal bonding. They are typically made of stretchy fabric, providing a snug and secure hold. On the other hand, hip wraps are designed to carry the baby on the caregiver's hip, providing a convenient and comfortable option for quick in and out movements without the need for complex wrapping techniques. Both front and hip wraps offer a hands-free and ergonomic solution for caregivers to carry their babies.</p></p>
