<p><p>Automotive wastegate valves are crucial components in turbocharged engines to control boost pressure. There are two types of wastegate valves commonly used in the market - internal wastegate and external wastegate. Internal wastegates are built into the turbocharger housing, making them more compact and cost-effective. On the other hand, external wastegates are separate units mounted outside the turbocharger, offering better control over boost pressure. Both types play a crucial role in optimizing engine performance and efficiency in turbocharged vehicles.</p></p>
