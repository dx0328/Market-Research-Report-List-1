<p><p>Military Aircraft Stand Entry Guidance System market is divided into two types: Visual Docking Guidance System (VDGS) and Advanced Visual Docking Guidance System (AVDGS). VDGS provides pilots with visual aids in order to safely park and dock their aircrafts. It includes features such as laser guidance systems and autonomous docking capability. On the other hand, AVDGS takes the technology a step further by incorporating advanced sensor technologies like cameras and radars to enhance the precision and safety of the docking process. Both systems play a crucial role in efficiently managing military aircraft parking and docking operations.</p></p>
