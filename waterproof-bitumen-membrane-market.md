<p><p>Waterproof Bitumen Membrane market can be divided into two main types: SBS Type and APP Type. SBS Type membranes are modified with Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene polymers, providing enhanced flexibility and elongation properties. On the other hand, APP Type membranes are modified with Atactic Polypropylene, offering improved temperature resistance and UV stability. Both types of membranes are widely used in the construction industry for waterproofing applications, providing durable and reliable protection against water infiltration.</p></p>
