<p><p>Brand Protection and Security Labels are used to safeguard products from counterfeiting and theft. Non-residue security labels leave a visible void message when tampered with, indicating product authenticity. Dual layer security labels employ a complex construction with hidden patterns or texts, which are only visible upon tampering. Similarly, security tape ensures packaging integrity by exposing a void message upon removal. These market types provide companies with effective tools to protect their brands and prevent unauthorized access, deterring potential criminals and maintaining consumer trust in the authenticity and quality of their products.</p></p>
