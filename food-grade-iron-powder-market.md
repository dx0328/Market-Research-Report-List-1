<p><p>Food Grade Iron Powder Market includes two main types - Elemental Iron and Iron Compounds. Elemental Iron refers to pure iron in its metallic form, which is used for fortification of food products due to its high bioavailability. Iron Compounds, on the other hand, are combinations of iron with other elements, such as ferrous sulfate or ferric pyrophosphate, which are also commonly used as food fortificants. Both forms of iron play a crucial role in addressing iron deficiency and improving overall nutritional health in consumers.</p></p>
