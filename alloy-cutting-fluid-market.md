<p><p>Alloy cutting fluids are used during metal cutting operations to lubricate and cool the cutting tool and workpiece. There are two main types of alloy cutting fluids: oil-based and water-based. Oil-based cutting fluids are typically used for heavy-duty machining operations and provide excellent lubrication and heat dissipation. Water-based cutting fluids are more environmentally friendly and are preferred for light to medium machining operations. Both types of cutting fluids have their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific machining requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
