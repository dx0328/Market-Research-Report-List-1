<p><p>Manual Transmission Fluid (MTF) is available in three main types - Mineral MTF, Synthetic MTF, and Semi-Synthetic MTF. Mineral MTF is made from conventional mineral oils, providing basic protection and lubrication for manual transmissions. Synthetic MTF is produced from high-quality synthetic oils, offering superior thermal stability and performance. Semi-Synthetic MTF combines the benefits of both mineral and synthetic oils, providing a balance of protection and performance. Consumers can choose the type of MTF based on their specific needs and vehicle requirements.</p></p>
