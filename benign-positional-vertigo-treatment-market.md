The main treatment option for BPPV is a series of head-positioning maneuvers called canalith repositioning procedures (CRPs), also known as the Epley maneuver. These maneuvers aim to reposition the calcium crystals that have become dislodged in the inner ear, causing the vertigo symptoms.</p><p>The BPPV treatment market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5% during the forecast period. Anti-cholinergics work by blocking the action of acetylcholine, a neurotransmitter that plays a role in dizziness.
