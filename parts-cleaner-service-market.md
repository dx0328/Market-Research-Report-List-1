<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water/Aqueous-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The parts cleaner service market can be classified into two main types: solvent-based and water/aqueous-based. Solvent-based cleaners use chemical solvents to remove grease, oil, and other contaminants from parts and equipment. These solvents can be petroleum-based or derived from other compounds. On the other hand, water/aqueous-based cleaners rely on the power of water to clean the parts. These cleaners often contain additives that enhance their cleaning capabilities. Both types of cleaners aim to effectively remove dirt and grime, but they differ in the composition of the cleaning agents used.</p></p>
