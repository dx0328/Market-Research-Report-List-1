<p><p>Laparoscopic Cannulas are medical devices used in minimally invasive surgeries. They come in two main types: Disposable Cannulas and Reusable Cannulas. </p><p>Disposable Cannulas are intended for single-use only and are discarded after each procedure, reducing the risk of infection and contamination. </p><p>On the other hand, Reusable Cannulas are sterilized and used multiple times, making them more cost-effective in the long run.</p><p>Both types play a crucial role in laparoscopic procedures by providing access to the surgical site and facilitating the insertion of instruments.</p></p>
