<p><strong>The Respiratory Monitoring Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulse Oximeter</li><li>Peak Flow Meter</li><li>Carbon Dioxide Analyzer</li><li>Spirometer</li><li>Polysomnography</li><li>Gas Analyzer</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Respiratory monitoring equipment refers to various devices used to measure and evaluate respiratory parameters. Pulse oximeter measures oxygen saturation in the blood, while peak flow meter assesses the air flowing through lungs. Carbon dioxide analyzer monitors the levels of carbon dioxide during respiration, and spirometer measures lung capacity and airflow. Polysomnography monitors sleep patterns and respiratory disturbances during sleep. Gas analyzers measure the concentration of specific gases in the respiratory system. Finally, other respiratory monitoring equipment includes devices like respiratory rate monitors and capnographs, which measure respiratory rate and carbon dioxide levels, respectively.</p></p>
