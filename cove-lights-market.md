<p><p>Cove lights are a type of lighting fixture that is commonly used to provide ambient lighting in a room. There are two main types of cove lights on the market: recessed and surface mounted. Recessed cove lights are installed into the ceiling or wall so that they are flush with the surface, creating a seamless look. Surface mounted cove lights are installed directly onto the surface of the ceiling or wall, providing a more prominent design feature. Both types of cove lights offer different aesthetics and lighting effects to enhance a space.</p></p>
