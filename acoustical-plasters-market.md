<p><p>Acoustical plasters are categorized into various types based on the design and pattern of holes in the plaster surface. These types include round hole type, square hole type, slotted hole type, and others. Round hole type plasters have small circular holes, square hole type plasters have square-shaped holes, and slotted hole type plasters have elongated slots. The different designs and patterns of these holes help in absorbing sound and reducing noise levels, making acoustical plasters suitable for use in various commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
