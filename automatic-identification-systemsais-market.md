<p><p>Automatic Identification Systems (AIS) are used in maritime communication for vessel tracking and safety. The AIS market consists of different types, including Class A AIS, Class B AIS, and AIS Base Stations. Class A AIS is mandatory for commercial vessels and provides accurate data on vessel identification, position, course, and speed. Class B AIS is used by smaller vessels and leisure boats and transmits less frequent and less detailed information. AIS Base Stations receive and display AIS data, enabling enhanced vessel traffic management and monitoring capabilities for maritime authorities and operators.</p></p>
