<p><p>Femoral Arterial Cannulae are medical devices used in cardiac surgeries to provide access to the femoral artery for delivering blood to the heart-lung machine. The Unipolar Femoral Arterial Cannulae market involves cannulae with a single lumen for blood flow, while the Bipolar Femoral Arterial Cannulae market consists of cannulae with two lumens for simultaneous blood flow and pressure monitoring. Both types of cannulae play a vital role in ensuring proper blood circulation and oxygenation during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
