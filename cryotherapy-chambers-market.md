<p><ul><li>Vertical Cryotherapy Chambers</li><li>Horizontal Cryotherapy Chambers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryotherapy chambers are used to expose the body to extremely low temperatures for medical or therapeutic purposes. Vertical cryotherapy chambers are designed for standing and fully immerse the body in cold air, ensuring even distribution. On the other hand, horizontal cryotherapy chambers allow the patient to lie down and usually have an open top, allowing the head to remain outside the chamber. Both types provide similar benefits, such as treating inflammation, enhancing athletic performance, and relieving pain, but cater to different preferences and space requirements.</p></p>
