The market for GGT testing includes two main types: ELISA Based and Colorimetric Based. ELISA Based testing utilizes the Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay technique to detect and quantify GGT levels in a sample. On the other hand, Colorimetric Based testing relies on measuring color changes in a reaction between GGT and specific substrates. Both methods provide accurate and reliable results, allowing healthcare professionals to effectively monitor liver health and diagnose liver conditions.</p></p>
