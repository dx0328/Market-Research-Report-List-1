<p><strong>The Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure-Sensitive Adhesives</li><li>Reactive Adhesives</li><li>Drying Adhesives</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesives market is divided into various types based on their properties and applications. Pressure-sensitive adhesives are tacky at room temperature and require only light pressure to form a bond. Reactive adhesives require a chemical reaction to form a bond and are often used in high-stress applications. Drying adhesives typically harden through evaporation of a solvent. Other markets include hot melt adhesives, structural adhesives, and sealants, each with their own unique properties and applications in industries such as automotive, construction, and packaging.</p></p>
