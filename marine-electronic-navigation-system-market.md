<p><strong>The Marine Electronic Navigation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vector Chart (ENC)</li><li>Raster Chart (RNC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine electronic navigation systems are categorized into two main types: Vector Chart (ENC) and Raster Chart (RNC) markets. Vector charts use geometric shapes to display navigational data, offering scalability and easy updating. Meanwhile, raster charts are digital images of paper charts, providing a visual representation of navigational information. Both types have their own advantages and are widely used in the marine industry for safe and efficient navigation on the water.</p></p>
