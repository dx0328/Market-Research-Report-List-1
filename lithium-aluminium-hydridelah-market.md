<p><p>The Lithium Aluminium Hydride (LAH) market includes two main types of production methods - the Schlesinger Method and the High-pressure Synthesis Method. The Schlesinger Method involves the reaction of lithium hydride with aluminium chloride at low temperatures, while the High-pressure Synthesis Method uses high pressures and temperatures to directly synthesize LAH from lithium and aluminium. Both methods have their own advantages and limitations, and are used in different industries depending on specific requirements and applications.</p></p>
