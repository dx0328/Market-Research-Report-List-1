<p><p>Transformer monitoring software market is segmented into various types to address different aspects of transformer performance and health. DGA software monitors dissolved gas analysis to detect potential issues in the transformer oil. Bushing monitoring software focuses on monitoring the condition of transformer bushings. Partial discharge (PD) software detects and analyzes partial discharges in transformers to prevent failures. Other types of software may include monitoring for temperature, moisture, and vibration, providing a comprehensive overview of the transformer's health.</p></p>
