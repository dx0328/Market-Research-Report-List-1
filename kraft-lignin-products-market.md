<p><strong>The Kraft Lignin Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Softwood Kraft Lignin</li><li>Hardwood Kraft Lignin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kraft lignin products refer to lignin extracted from the pulping process of wood. Softwood Kraft lignin is derived from softwood trees, such as pine and spruce, while hardwood Kraft lignin comes from hardwood trees like birch and oak. Softwood Kraft lignin is known for its high purity and light color, making it ideal for applications in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic industries. Hardwood Kraft lignin, on the other hand, is valued for its higher molecular weight and strength, making it suitable for use in adhesives and composites.</p></p>
