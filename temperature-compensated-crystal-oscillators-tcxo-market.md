<p><p>Temperature-compensated crystal oscillators (TCXOs) come in two main market types - SMD shape and PIN shape. SMD shape TCXOs are surface-mount devices that are compact and easy to integrate into PCBs, making them suitable for space-constrained applications. On the other hand, PIN shape TCXOs have through-hole pins for secure mounting on the circuit board, providing stability and reliability in high-vibration environments. Both types offer excellent frequency stability over temperature variations, making them ideal for precision timing applications in various industries.</p></p>
