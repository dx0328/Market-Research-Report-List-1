<p><ul><li>Closed Circuit</li><li>Semi-Closed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rebreathers for diving can be divided into two main market types: closed circuit and semi-closed. Closed circuit rebreathers recycle the diver's exhaled breath by scrubbing carbon dioxide and replenishing oxygen, allowing for longer dives and reduced gas consumption. Semi-closed rebreathers release a portion of the exhaled breath into the water while still recycling a portion, providing a balance between gas efficiency and added safety. Both types offer benefits for different diving scenarios, with closed circuit rebreathers typically being favored for technical and deep diving, while semi-closed rebreathers are popular for recreational diving.</p></p>
