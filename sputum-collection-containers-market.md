<p><p>Sputum collection containers are available in two main types: plastic and glass. Plastic containers are popular due to their lightweight, durability, and cost-effectiveness. Glass containers, on the other hand, are reusable and have the advantage of being transparent, allowing for easy examination of the sample. However, they are heavier and more prone to breakage.
