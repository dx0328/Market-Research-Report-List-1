<p><ul><li>Rigid Cable Conduit</li><li>Flexible Cable Conduit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cable conduits are available in two main types - rigid cable conduit and flexible cable conduit. </p><p>Rigid cable conduits are made of sturdy materials like PVC, steel, or aluminum and provide strong protection for cables in harsh environments. They are ideal for fixed installations where cables need to be well-protected and secured.</p><p>Flexible cable conduits, on the other hand, are made of materials like PVC, nylon, or corrugated metal that allow for easy bending and installation in tight or curved spaces. They are more versatile and adaptable for applications where flexibility is required.</p></p>
