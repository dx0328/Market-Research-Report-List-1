<p><ul><li>Water-Based</li><li>Oil-Gel Based</li><li>Foam-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fracking chemicals and fluids are categorized into three main types: water-based, oil-gel based, and foam-based. Water-based fluids are the most commonly used, consisting primarily of water and various additives. Oil-gel based fluids contain gelling agents to thicken the fluid for better proppant suspension. Foam-based fluids are composed of gas and a foaming agent, creating a lighter mixture that reduces friction. Each type of fluid has its own unique properties and benefits, catering to specific needs in the fracking process.</p></p>
