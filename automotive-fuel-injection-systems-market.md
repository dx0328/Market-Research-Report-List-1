<p><ul><li>Gasoline Port Injection</li><li>Gasoline Direct Injection</li><li>Diesel Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Fuel Injection Systems market includes three main types: Gasoline Port Injection, Gasoline Direct Injection, and Diesel Direct Injection. Gasoline Port Injection systems inject fuel into the intake ports of the engine using fuel injectors. Gasoline Direct Injection systems deliver fuel directly into the combustion chamber, resulting in improved fuel efficiency and performance. Diesel Direct Injection systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber of diesel engines, providing better fuel efficiency and lower emissions. Each type of fuel injection system offers unique advantages and is used in various vehicles based on specific requirements.</p></p>
