<p><p>Dental bonding agents and adhesives play a crucial role in restorative dentistry by facilitating the adhesion of restorative materials to tooth structure. The market for these products can be categorized into three main types: total-etch bonding agents, self-etch bonding agents, and universal bonding agents. Total-etch bonding agents involve etching the tooth surface before applying the adhesive, while self-etch bonding agents eliminate the need for separate etching. Universal bonding agents can be used in various bonding techniques, making them versatile and convenient for dental practitioners.</p></p>
