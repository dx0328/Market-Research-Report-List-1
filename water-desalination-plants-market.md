<p><strong>The Water Desalination Plants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reverse osmosis (RO)</li><li>Multi-stage flash distillation (MSF)</li><li>Multiple-effect distillation (MED)</li><li>Hybrid</li><li>Electrodialysis (ED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water desalination plants utilize various technologies to remove salt and impurities from seawater to make it suitable for drinking and industrial use. The market types include reverse osmosis (RO), which uses pressure to separate salt from water; multi-stage flash distillation (MSF), which heats seawater to produce steam and then condenses it back into fresh water; multiple-effect distillation (MED), which uses multiple heating and cooling stages; hybrid systems that combine different technologies; and electrodialysis (ED), which uses an electric current to separate ions from water.</p></p>
