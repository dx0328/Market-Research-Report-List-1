<p><p>The optical interconnect market consists of various types of technologies and products. Cable assemblies include optical fibers enclosed within a protective jacket, serving as the backbone of optical communication systems. Connectors enable the connection of these cables, ensuring efficient transmission. Silicon photonics systems use silicon-based devices for high-speed data transfer. PIC-based interconnects utilize photonic integrated circuits for efficient signal routing. Optical engines are modules that integrate key optical components. Optical transceivers convert electrical signals to optical and vice versa. Free space optics use beams of light for communication. Fibers and waveguides guide and transmit optical signals within a system.</p></p>
