<p><p>Sandwich glass, also known as composite glass, is a type of glass that consists of multiple layers sandwiched together to create a stronger and more durable glass product. Smart glass, also known as switchable glass, can change its appearance in response to external stimuli, such as light, heat, or voltage. As the demand for smart glass continues to rise, it is expected to drive further growth in the sandwich glass market in the coming years.</p></p>
