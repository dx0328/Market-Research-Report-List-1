<p><strong>The Switching Regulators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Buck Regulators (Step-Down)</li><li>Boost Regulators (Step-Up)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Switching regulators are electronic circuits that efficiently convert voltage from one level to another. Buck regulators, also known as step-down regulators, lower the input voltage to a lower output voltage. Boost regulators, or step-up regulators, increase the input voltage to a higher output voltage. Both types are commonly used in various electronic devices to regulate power consumption. Buck regulators are suitable for applications where the output voltage needs to be lower than the input voltage, while boost regulators are ideal for applications requiring higher output voltage levels.</p></p>
