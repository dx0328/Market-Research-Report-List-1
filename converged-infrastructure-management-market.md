<p><strong>The Converged Infrastructure Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fabric architecture integrated the system</li><li>Workload integrated system</li><li>Reference architecture integrated systems</li><li>Infrastructure component integrated the system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Converged Infrastructure Management market comprises four types. Firstly, fabric architecture integrated systems combine computing, networking, and storage resources into a single platform for streamlined management. Secondly, workload integrated systems are designed for specific applications or workloads, optimizing performance and simplifying management. Thirdly, reference architecture integrated systems provide predefined blueprints for building a converged infrastructure, ensuring compatibility and simplifying deployment. Finally, infrastructure component integrated systems allow organizations to integrate their existing infrastructure components into a unified platform, enhancing scalability and management efficiency.</p></p>
