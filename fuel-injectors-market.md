<p><strong>The Fuel Injectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multiport Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel injectors are common components in modern automotive engines. Throttle body injection systems deliver fuel directly into the throttle body, located at the entrance to the engine manifold. Multiport injection systems inject fuel into individual cylinders through separate injectors. Direct injection systems inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber. Each type of fuel injection system offers unique advantages in terms of fuel efficiency, power output, and emissions control. The market for fuel injectors is driven by the demand for advanced fuel delivery systems in the automotive industry.</p></p>
