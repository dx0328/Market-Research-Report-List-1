<p><ul><li>EVA HMA</li><li>SBC HMA</li><li>PA HMA</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hot melt adhesives for packaging are categorized into different types based on their compositions. EVA HMA, or Ethylene Vinyl Acetate Hot Melt Adhesives, are commonly used for general packaging applications due to their flexibility and strength. SBC HMA, or Styrenic Block Copolymer Hot Melt Adhesives, offer excellent adhesion to various substrates. PA HMA, or Polyamide Hot Melt Adhesives, are known for their heat resistance and are often used for high-temperature packaging. The "Others" category includes a range of specialty hot melt adhesives tailored for specific packaging needs.</p></p>
