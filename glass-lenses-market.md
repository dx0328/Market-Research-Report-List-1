<p><strong>The Glass Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Progressive lens</li><li>Single vision lens</li><li>Bifocal lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Glass lenses market can be categorized into three main types: Progressive lens, Single vision lens, and Bifocal lens. Progressive lenses provide a seamless transition between different focal distances, making them ideal for individuals with presbyopia. Single vision lenses correct vision at a single distance, such as for near or far vision. Bifocal lenses contain two separate prescriptions in one lens, typically one for distance vision and one for near vision. Each type of lens caters to different vision needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
