<p><p>School administrative software refers to computer programs that help in managing various administrative tasks in schools. The market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises software. Cloud-based software operates over the internet and allows users to access and manage data remotely, offering flexibility and scalability. On the other hand, on-premises software is installed on local servers and requires users to have physical access to the system. Both types have their own advantages and considerations, and schools can choose based on their specific needs and IT infrastructure.</p></p>
