<p><p>Fused Silica Microlens Arrays are optical components used in various applications such as imaging, laser beam shaping, and fiber optics. There are two main types of Microlens Arrays: Aspherical Microlens Array and Spherical Microlens Array. Aspherical Microlens Arrays have non-spherical surfaces that help correct optical aberrations, while Spherical Microlens Arrays have curved surfaces with a constant radius of curvature. Both types offer unique advantages depending on the specific application requirements, making them versatile choices for different optical systems.</p></p>
