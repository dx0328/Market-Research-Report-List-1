<p><p>Disposable Anesthesia Video Laryngoscope Market includes two main types: Channeled Video Laryngoscopes and Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes. Channeled Video Laryngoscopes have a built-in channel to guide the endotracheal tube into the airway during intubation, making it easier for healthcare professionals to visualize the vocal cords. On the other hand, Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes do not have a dedicated channel, requiring a separate technique to guide the endotracheal tube into the airway. Both types offer disposable options for single-use applications in anesthesia procedures.</p></p>
