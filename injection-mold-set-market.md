<p><p>The injection mold set market comprises two main types: aluminium molds and steel molds. Aluminium molds are made from aluminum material, offering advantages such as lightweight design, faster cooling times, and lower production costs. They are commonly used in low-volume production due to their lower durability. On the other hand, steel molds are made from steel material, providing superior durability, higher production volumes, and longer tool life. Steel molds are preferred for high-volume production but tend to be more expensive compared to aluminium molds.</p></p>
