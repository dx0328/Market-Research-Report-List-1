<p><p>Laminar Flow Hoods are used in various industries to provide a controlled clean environment. Horizontal Laminar Flow Hoods have airflow moving horizontally across the work surface, while Vertical Laminar Flow Hoods have airflow moving vertically downwards. Each type is designed for specific applications, with Horizontal Hoods being preferred for applications where contamination control is critical, and Vertical Hoods being suitable for applications requiring protection from hazardous materials. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining a sterile work environment in industries such as pharmaceuticals, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
