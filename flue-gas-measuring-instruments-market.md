<p><strong>The Flue Gas Measuring Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Flue Gas Measuring</li><li>Non-continuous Flue Gas Measuring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Continuous Flue Gas Measuring Instruments are designed to monitor and analyze gas emissions in real-time, providing continuous data on the quality of exhaust gases. This type of monitoring is ideal for industries with high emission levels that require constant monitoring to ensure compliance with regulations.</p><p>Non-continuous Flue Gas Measuring Instruments, on the other hand, are used for periodic measurements of gas emissions, typically during maintenance checks or inspections. While they do not provide real-time data like continuous instruments, they are still important for ensuring that emissions remain within acceptable levels.</p></p>
