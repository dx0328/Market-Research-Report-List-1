<p><p>The disposable microcatheter market includes two types: straight microcatheters and curved microcatheters. Straight microcatheters have a linear shape and are designed for straightforward applications, where the catheter can be inserted directly into the desired area. On the other hand, curved microcatheters have a pre-shaped curvature to navigate through anatomical structures with more complexity, reaching challenging areas. Both types are designed for single-use purposes and play a crucial role in various medical procedures like diagnostic imaging, angiography, embolization, and delivering therapeutic agents.</p></p>
