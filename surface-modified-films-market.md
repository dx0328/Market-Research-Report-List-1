<p><p>Surface modified films can be categorized into two main types: transparent and metallized films. Transparent films are clear and allow light to pass through, making them suitable for various applications such as protective packaging and labels. Metallized films, on the other hand, are coated with a thin layer of metal to provide properties such as enhanced barrier protection and a reflective surface. These films find use in applications such as food packaging, insulation, and decorative purposes.</p></p>
