<p><p>The Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System market is categorized into three types: Infrared Type, Time-of-Flight Type, and Stereoscopic Vision Type. The Infrared Type utilizes infrared sensors to detect and count passengers as they enter or exit a vehicle. The Time-of-Flight Type employs laser pulses to measure the distance and calculate the number of passengers. The Stereoscopic Vision Type uses multiple cameras to capture depth information and accurately count passengers. These systems enable transportation authorities to automate passenger counting processes and enhance efficiency in public transportation systems.</p></p>
