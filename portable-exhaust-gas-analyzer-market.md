<p><p>The Portable Exhaust Gas Analyzer market includes various types of analyzers such as NDIR Absorption Method, Electrochemical Method, Hydrogen Flame Ionization Method, and Chemiluminescence Method. NDIR Absorption Method measures gas concentration by analyzing the absorption of infrared light, Electrochemical Method uses chemical reactions to detect gases, Hydrogen Flame Ionization Method measures hydrocarbons using a hydrogen flame, and Chemiluminescence Method detects gases through the emission of light during chemical reactions. Each method offers specific advantages and is used in different applications based on their capabilities.</p></p>
