Potassium-based fertilizers help plants withstand stress and improve overall health. Calcium fertilizers contribute to cell wall strength and nutrient uptake. Micronutrient fertilizers provide essential trace elements for plant growth and development. Each type of fertilizer nutrient serves a unique function in promoting healthy plant growth.</p></p>
