<p><p>Roller seam welding machines are divided into different types based on the direction of the seam being welded. Circumferential seam welding machines are used to weld round objects such as pipes and cylinders. Longitudinal seam welding machines are used for welding seams in a straight line. Corner seam welding machines are specifically designed for welding seams at corners. Other types of roller seam welding machines cater to specific welding requirements that do not fit into the aforementioned categories. Each type of machine is designed to meet different welding needs efficiently.</p></p>
