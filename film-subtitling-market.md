<p><p>Film subtitling refers to the process of translating dialogue and other relevant information into text displayed on screen. Native language subtitling involves translating the dialogue of a film into the language spoken by the target audience. Foreign language subtitling entails translating the dialogue from a foreign language into the native language of the target audience. Minority language subtitling focuses on translating films into languages spoken by smaller, specialized communities. Special language subtitling pertains to translating films that contain specific technical or jargon-heavy content into a language that the target audience can understand.</p></p>
