<p><p>Wooden pallet collars come in two main types: foldable and unfoldable. Foldable collars are designed to collapse flat when not in use, making them ideal for saving storage space and transportation costs. On the other hand, unfoldable collars are fixed and do not collapse flat, providing more stability and security for stacked goods. Both types cater to different market needs, with foldable collars being favored for their convenience and unfoldable collars for their robustness and durability.</p></p>
