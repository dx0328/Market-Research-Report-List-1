Reverse Osmosis (RO) Membrane is used for desalination and purification, Nanofiltration (NF) Membrane removes divalent ions and small molecules, Forward Osmosis (FO) Membrane is used for low-pressure processes, and Electrodialysis (ED) Membrane separates ions using an electric field. Each type of membrane caters to different needs in brackish water treatment and purification processes.</p></p>
