Peptide hormones are substances that regulate various physiological functions and are used as therapeutic drugs. Blood products are derived from human blood and can include factors necessary for blood clotting or immune response. Enzymes are proteins that speed up chemical reactions in the body and can be used as therapeutic agents for various conditions.</p></p>
