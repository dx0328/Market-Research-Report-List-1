<p><p>Infusion Bottle Polypropylene (PP) is a commonly used material in the healthcare sector due to its durability and chemical resistance. In the market, there are two main types of PP for infusion bottle manufacturing: PP-H and PP-R. PP-H, or homopolymer PP, is known for its high strength and thermal resistance, making it ideal for medical devices that require robust performance. On the other hand, PP-R, or random copolymer PP, offers improved impact resistance and flexibility, making it suitable for applications where durability and flexibility are key factors.</p></p>
