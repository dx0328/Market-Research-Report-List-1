<p><ul><li>On-Pump Transfusion Device</li><li>Off-Pump Transfusion Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cardiopulmonary auto-transfusion systems are medical devices used during cardiac surgeries to collect and reinfuse the patient's own blood, reducing the need for donor blood transfusions. There are two main types of these systems: on-pump transfusion devices, which are integrated into the heart-lung machine during surgery; and off-pump transfusion devices, which are used separately from the heart-lung machine. Both types aim to minimize the risk of transfusion reactions and infections while improving patient outcomes during cardiac procedures.</p></p>
